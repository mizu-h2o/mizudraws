TESTIMONIALS

" Viego's artistic approach, brought together under the title The Cosmic Egg, does not fit into the classic format of a banal exhibition. It is rather an ample, well-cohesive visual and philosophical discourse, built with a conceptual finesse that is rarely found. The project does not aim to affirm out loud, but to suggest; it does not offer conclusions, but opens spaces for thought, where form, gesture and matter become instruments of plastic reflection. Now, when the image is often reduced to effect and spectacle, and art is fragmented into quick and easily digestible stories, The Cosmic Egg invites us to a return to the beginnings – not as a flight into the past, but as a lucid rediscovery of the original: that point where the visible and the invisible, science and myth, form and meaning meet silently in a creative gesture."

Art Chronicle in TIME Magazine by Petruta Luchian

" Thank you so much for all the work you put into the book cover! I don’t think anything would make me happier than when those who read my prose understand it on a deeper level. You captured the essence of the story “The Day of the Great Revelation” and “told” this story through a bold, evocative, and colorful cover. I sincerely thank you and hope we’ll meet in person someday, somewhere."

Author of The Day of the Great Revelation
